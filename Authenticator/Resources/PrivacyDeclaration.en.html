Privacy declaration Authenticator for Windows

General

This is the privacy declaration of Authenticator for Windows. With all respect for your privacy, we try to limit the required data to a minimal amount. We always adhere to the rules set by the Data Protection Act. For all permissions the app uses, we explain why it's being used.

This statement may be amended unilaterally at any time.

Camera

The camera in your device can be used to scan codes. While scanning, there will be searched for a QR code to read. This will always be processed locally and will NEVER be sent over the internet.

Internet connection

Your internet connection is being used to retrieve the most precise time from an NTP server. You can turn this off at any time to prevent data usage.
